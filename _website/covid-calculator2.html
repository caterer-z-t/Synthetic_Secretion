COVID-19 Testing Group

The COVID-19 Testing Group is a community resource for sharing COVID-19 prevalence, seroprevalence, and burden studies, as well as planning tools and data. This website is maintained by the Larremore Lab at the University of Colorado, Boulder and the Grad Lab at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health.
